About Us :-

Our bee stock consists entirely of indigenous Asian Honey Bees and stingless bees, which do not need artificial support to survive or produce. They are kept in apiaries in untainted forage-rich tropical forests, far away from pollution and the attrition of winter, allowing them to produce honey naturally almost all year round. To keep the innate foraging instincts of our bees, which ensures that the honey that they produce are as natural as can be and contains the nutrients of the forest, we have a strict policy of not feeding them.
